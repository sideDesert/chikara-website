Programs

At Chikara, we offer a variety of programs tailored to different age groups and goals. All our programs are available in group classes and one-on-one sessions.

KIDS PROGRAM

(3-16 ages)

Our kids' karate program is designed to help young practitioners develop the physical, social mental skills required to conquer today's world.

ADULT PROGRAM

(16+ ages)

Our adult program caters to practitioners of all levels, from beginners to advanced. We focus on providing tailored programs to their goals.

FEMALE-ONLY CLASSES

Our women-only classes provide a comfortable environment to: Learn practical self-defence techniques Improve fitness and strength Build confidence and empowerment Join a supportive community of like-minded women

Whether you're a beginner or an advanced practitioner, our flexible program structure allows you to choose the path that best fits your goals and schedule!
